<h2>Usage</h2>
<ol>
   <li>Launch the application</li>
   <li>Click <strong>Start Recording</strong></li>
   <li>Use your computer normally</li>
   <li>Click <strong>Stop Recording</strong></li>
   <li>The video will be processed automatically with zoom effects applied to your clicks</li>
</ol>
